We record decades of thoughtful and bold demands for racial equity written by students, faculty, and staff at the University of Virginia.

Responding to faculty, student, and community activist calls for concrete and immediate action towards racial equity following the murder of George Floyd in May 2020, UVA President Jim Ryan asked the Racial Equity Taskforce to create thoughtful and bold recommendations for racial equity at the University. But thoughtful and bold recommendations already exist. The demands made by students, faculty, and community members through protests, petitions, and working groups represent decades spent on this very task.

de·mand /dəˈmand/ n.
1simply a recommendation made by a person/people who have become angry with complacency.

To credit those activists and to contextualize the present moment, we compiled 50 years of demand documents, analyzed them for common themes, and are opening them up through an interactive library format.
